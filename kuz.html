How to Make a Clarinet?

Materials:

  • Pads
  • Wood
  • Reed
    • You have to wet the reeds somehow
      1. Put the reed to oven with water
      2. Heat the water
      3. Wait about 5 min
    • you should pick the right thickness of reed

How to Play a Clarinet

  1. Put your reed to the mouthpiece
  2. Place the parts of clarinet
  3. Take a breath and blow
